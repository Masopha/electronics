Company History

Founded in 1995, our company began as a small retail store selling electronics in the heart of downtown. Our mission was to provide customers with the latest and greatest technology at affordable prices. As we grew in popularity, we expanded our inventory to include a wider range of products, from computers and phones to home entertainment systems and gaming consoles. Over the years, we have established ourselves as a trusted name in the electronics industry, with a loyal customer base and a reputation for excellent customer service. In 2010, we opened our first online store, allowing us to reach customers around the world

Smart Electronics Aims

  • Provide customers with the latest and most innovative technology products.
  • Offer competitive pricing and deals to ensure affordability for all customers.
  • Deliver exceptional customer service and support to create a positive shopping experience.

Smart Electronics Objectives

  • Increase online sales revenue by 20% within the next fiscal year through targeted marketing and advertising campaigns
  • Expand product offerings to include a line of eco-friendly and sustainable electronics to meet growing customer demand for environmentally responsible products
  • Improve customer satisfaction ratings by 10% through enhanced employee training programs and streamlined customer service processes
